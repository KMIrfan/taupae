Seven decades of excellence

1959, India

In 1959, P. K. Aboobacker founded the TIPTOP Department Store in Tamil Nadu, India. Over the course of 25 years, the group experienced remarkable growth, expanding its presence to a network of 25 showrooms across four states in South India. The beloved neighborhood department stores are a cherished destination, offering an array of top-quality leather goods, shoes, bags, and an assortment of other must-have items.

1980, UAE

In 1980, TIPTOP Department Store expanded its presence to the UAE and concurrently established a manufacturing unit named YASMIN PLASTIC INDUSTRIES CO. LLC. Renowned as pioneers in the manufacturing and trading of bags, the comprehensive product range encompasses Travel Goods, Sports Bags, School Bags, Laptop Bags, Conference Bags, Wheel Covers, Banners, Toilet Pouches, Cosmetic Pouches, Duffel Bags, Shopping Bags, and more.

Your Story in Bespoke Leather

From hand-stitching to precision detailing, every nuance is carefully considered, resulting in bespoke leather masterpieces that not only exude refinement but also bear the distinctive mark of unparalleled craftsmanship. Spanning carpentry to leather craftsmanship and extending to organizational office decor, our seasoned artisans deliver an unmatched level of innovation, craftsmanship, and post-production service.

Our dedication isn't confined to a specific industry segment; instead, it revolves around distinctive, forward-thinking lifestyle projects that ignite trends and establish cutting-edge standards for guest spaces globally. This commitment to excellence transforms raw materials into timeless pieces, where the beauty of the handmade process is celebrated, ensuring that each product tells a unique story of skill, passion, and enduring quality.
